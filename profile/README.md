# Hacker Valley Media

Cybersecurity storytelling through podcasts, video, and code.

We started Hacker Valley because the cybersecurity industry has incredible people doing incredible work — and most of them never get heard. Since 2019, we've been changing that. Over 400 episodes, 50,000+ monthly listeners across 150+ countries, and a team that knows cybersecurity because we lived it.

Hacker Valley Media is a full-service creative agency built by cyber practitioners, for cybersecurity brands. We produce podcasts, video series, conference coverage, and social content — the whole operation, not just the mic.

## What We Do

**[Hacker Valley Studio](https://hackervalley.com)** — Our flagship podcast. Real conversations with the people behind cybersecurity. No scripts. No fluff.

**Content Operations** — We run full content programs for cybersecurity companies. Podcasts, video, social, events. Your outsourced content team at a fraction of the cost of building in-house.

**Conference Coverage** — RSA, Black Hat, and beyond. On-the-ground production crews capturing the stories that matter.

## Open Source

We build tools that make our work better — and we share them.

- **[slop-browser](https://github.com/Hacker-Valley-Media/slop-browser)** — Browser automation CLI for AI agents. No CDP, no API keys, no detection. A Chrome extension that controls your real browser from the inside. Passes every major bot detection suite as a normal human.
